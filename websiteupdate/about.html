ABOUT RAMONA DRIVE

Formed by students at **California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo**, Ramona Drive is a high-energy metalcore and rock inspired group bringing back the nostalgic intensity of the early 2000s scene. Blending aggressive riffs, passionate vocals, and intricate rhythms, the band aims to deliver a sound that is both a throwback and a fresh punch to the modern rock landscape.
